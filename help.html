Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I apply for a student visa?

You can apply for a student visa by submitting the required documents through our application portal.

What documents are required?

You need a passport, proof of admission, financial proof, and other supporting documents.

How long does the visa process take?

Processing times vary by country. It usually takes 4-8 weeks.
